Where: Puzhayoram International Convention Centre, Eravimangalam, Thrissur, Kerala.
When: 6:00 P.M. onwards

How to reach here:

By Air: Nearest Airport is in Cochin, which is 50 kms from the venue. You can hire a pre-paid taxi (approx Rs 1300). Or alternatively you can hop on to a KSRTC AC low floor bus to Thrissur from the Airport (Rs 350) which runs in every 2 hours.

By Train: Nearest Railway station is Thrissur railway station which is 10kms from the venue. Prepaid auto/Uber available outside the station (Rs 200)
